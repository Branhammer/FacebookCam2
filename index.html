A Lightweight Camera.

Featherweight Camera is an Android application that allows users to take pictures and quickly upload them to a facebook album. Once the app has uploaded an image it is deleted from the phone. The application preserves external storage for music and other files that are more important to store locally.

Current Progress

The app is functional. Users can take a bunch of pictures, login to Facebook, select an album and upload the photos there. They also have the option to move the photos to their local storage.

Application Limitations

The appication is far from perfect. Eventually I would like to include the following:

  • Batch editing prior to upload
  • Perhaps including Google or Dropbox support
  • Adding descriptions to the photos being uploaded
  • An interface overhaul, make it look nice
